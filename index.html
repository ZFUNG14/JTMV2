About the Festival

Where Malaysia
meets the world.

Journey to Malaysia is an annual cultural celebration organised by the Malaysian Student Association — a space where Malaysians away from home can gather, and where curious locals can discover a culture that's rich, vibrant, and incredibly warm.

Whether you're missing the taste of home, looking for your community, or just want to experience something genuinely special — this festival is for you.
