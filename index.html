About the Festival

Where Malaysia
meets the world.

Journey to Malaysia is a cultural celebration organised primarily by the Malaysian students associations in Perth as well as Perth's Consulate General of Malaysia. Journey to Malaysia is a space where Malaysians away from home can gather, and the greater Perth community can discover a culture that's rich, vibrant, and incredibly warm.

Whether you're missing the taste of home, looking for your community, or just want to experience something genuinely special, this festival is for you.
